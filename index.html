The Power of Persistent Prayer
Luke 18:1 - "Then Jesus told his disciples a parable to show them that they should always pray and not give up."
Prayer is not just a religious activity; it is a lifeline to our Creator. Jesus taught us to pray persistently, not because God is reluctant to answer, but because persistent prayer changes us. It builds our faith, deepens our dependence on God, and aligns our will with His.
Prayer: Lord, teach me to pray without ceasing. Give me the persistence to keep bringing my requests before You, trusting that You hear and will answer according to Your perfect will. Amen.
